Data Privacy and Storage
At InventoWare, we prioritize your data privacy and security. It's important to note that our Inventory Stock Management App exclusively utilizes local storage on your device (phone) for data storage and management. This means that your data is never transmitted to remote servers or shared with third parties. All your inventory data, including product details, stock levels, and reports, are stored locally on your device. Your data remains within the confines of your phone, offering an added layer of privacy and security.
